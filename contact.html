My Love for Weather!

My passion for weather started back when I was in 6th grade. I took a field trip to the Newseum in Washington D.C. and I was able to do a live broadcast of the local weather report on television. Since This experience, I have absolutely loved being on air and talking about weather. I am an aspiring meteorologist.

When I am not frantically looking at the local weather doplars or studying for my extreme weather class, I can be found at concerts, exploring new places, and living an adventurous lifestyle. Recently my love for attending live music and weather was brought together as I was able to assist artists at Atlantic Records predict the weather patterns for their shows.

I am always looking for friends to talk weather patterns with me! If you are interested in going storm chasing or just want to get to know me better, please reach out below!
